About Me

My Personal Background, Interests and Aspirations

I'm originally from New York City born and raised like both of my parents. I grew up watching and playing hockey, myself and my family are all New York Rangers fans. Since moving to Colorado I've become an avid college football fan especially with the addition of Deion Sanders as the head coach at CU. I also love to snowboard and have especially enjoyed the ample opportunity to do so since coming to Colorado. Some other hobbies of mine include fishing, playing golf and pool.

Academic Background and Goals

At CU Boulder I've focused on expanding my skills in the field of Computer Science by taking a variety of courses ranging from Data Science, to Software Development to Cyber Security. I've grown particularly fascinated with Data Science and especially Economics, through my Economics minor I gained a great understanding of micro and macroeconomics.

Professional Goals and Aspirations

Using my foundation of Computer Science and my skills within the field of Data Science, I hope to apply quantitative methods to real economic problems. I also hope to bridge economic theory with empirical analysis to derive valuable insights that can be communicated using clear visualizations and narratives. In the long term I'd like to specialize in an applied economic data science role where I'm able to design and deploy various predictive and forecasting models which help influence economic or business decision making. I'd also be interested in getting hands on participation or collaboration on some current research or publications.
